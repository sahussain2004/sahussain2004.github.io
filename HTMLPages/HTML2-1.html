Lists in HTML

Unordered Lists

These lists are used for items that do not have a particular order.
Types of Unordered Lists:
  • Disc
  • Circle
  • Square
  • None

Ordered Lists

These lists are used to emphasize the order of items.
Types of ordered lists:
    123
    ABC
    abc
    Roman Numerals
    Lowercase Roman

Nested Lists

We can place list inside another list to create a sublist.
  1. First Period: 8:20-9:48
  2. Second Period: 9:55-11:27
  3. Block Lunch
    1. A Block: 11:27-11:57
    2. B Block: 11:57-12:27
  4. Third Period: 12:32-2:00
  5. Fourth Period: 2:07-3:35
